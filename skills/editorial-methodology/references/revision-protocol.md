# Revision Protocol

## Three-Pass Revision

### Pass 1: Structural Edit
- Does the argument hold together?
- Are sections in the right order?
- Is anything missing? Is anything redundant?
- Does the opening hook work? Does the closing land?

### Pass 2: Line Edit
- Is every sentence clear and necessary?
- Are there jargon, clichés, or vague words?
- Is the rhythm varied (short/long sentences)?
- Are transitions between paragraphs smooth?

### Pass 3: Proofread
- Spelling, grammar, punctuation
- Consistent formatting (headings, lists, code blocks)
- Link integrity (do all links resolve?)
- Factual accuracy (names, dates, numbers)
