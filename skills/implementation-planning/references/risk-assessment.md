# Risk Assessment

## Risk Scoring

For each risk, score on two dimensions:
- **Probability:** Low (0-33%), Medium (34-66%), High (67-100%)
- **Impact:** Low (minor delay or rework), Medium (significant delay, 1-2 weeks), High (project-level impact, >2 weeks or production incident)

## Mitigations and Contingencies

| Risk Level | Action Required |
|-----------|----------------|
| High probability + High impact | Must have a mitigation AND a contingency |
| High probability + Medium impact | Must have a mitigation |
| Medium probability + High impact | Must have a mitigation |
| Low probability + High impact | Should have a contingency (insurance) |
| Low + Low | Document but no action required |

## Mitigation vs Contingency

- **Mitigation:** What you do NOW to reduce the probability or impact. Built into the plan.
- **Contingency:** What you do IF the risk materializes. Kept ready but not executed unless needed.

## Unmitigated Risks

A risk without a mitigation is not a risk — it's a known problem you've chosen not to address. Document why: is the mitigation cost too high? Is the impact acceptable? Is there a better mitigation that would be worth the cost?
